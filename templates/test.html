Welcome to SnipeIT-Framework

With SnipeIT-Framework, you can easily manage your assets and generate reports for your accounting software. Here are some of the features:

View changes made in SnipeIT

You can view the changes made in SnipeIT by navigating to the /changes route.

Generate xlsx reports for assets in SnipeIT

You can generate an xlsx report for assets in SnipeIT by navigating to the /xlsx_report route.

Create reports for assets in SnipeIT that you have in your accounting software

You can create a report for assets in SnipeIT that you have in your accounting software by navigating to the /asset_report route.

Report missing files in accounting software

You can report missing files in your accounting software that exist in SnipeIT by navigating to the /missing_files route.

Match files in SnipeIT and accounting software

You can match files in both SnipeIT and your accounting software by navigating to the /match_files route.

Create document for in-person acceptance of asset

You can create a document for in-person acceptance of an asset by navigating to the /acceptance_document route.
